The "Heat" Algorithm:

  1. Find maxNodes (country with most nodes).
  2. Calculate intensity = count / maxNodes.
  3. Radius Calculation:
    radius = 6px + (intensity * 11px)
    Ensures small nodes are visible, big nodes don't overlap too much.
